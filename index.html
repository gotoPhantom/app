How it protects you

End-to-end encryption

Only you and the person you’re talking to can read your messages. The server only ever sees scrambled data.

No phone number, no email

Your account is a nickname and the keys on your device. Nothing links it to your real identity.

Built to beat censorship

When the network blocks normal apps, PHANTOM automatically finds a path through — disguising its traffic as ordinary web browsing.

Your keys never leave your phone

Encryption keys are created and stay on your device. We could not read your messages even if we wanted to.

Messages live only on your device

They’re stored on your phone only; the server deletes them the moment they’re delivered.

Encrypted voice calls

Calls are end-to-end encrypted and disguised as an ordinary secure web connection.

Install in 4 steps

  1. Tap Download for Android to get the app file (an .apk).
  2. Open the downloaded file. Android may ask for permission to install from this source — allow it.
  3. Tap Install, then Open.
  4. Important: to update later, install the new file over the old app. Never uninstall — that would erase your account and chats.

Questions

Is it free?

Yes, completely. No subscription, no ads.

Do I need an invite or a code?

No — just download and start.

Is there an iPhone version?

Not yet — Android only for now.

What if I lose my phone?

Your identity lives in the keys on your device, so keep a backup from inside the app: Settings → Backup. Without a backup, a lost phone means a new identity.

Why isn’t it on Google Play?

To stay reachable where app stores are restricted, PHANTOM is installed directly from this page.
